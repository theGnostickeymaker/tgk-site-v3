  <p>When the two calendars realigned, it marked a cosmic rebirth. Cities fell silent. Fires were extinguished. The people waited. Then, with ritual precision, a new flame was lit. A new cycle began.</p>

  <p>This wasn&rsquo;t superstition. It was <strong>metaphysical engineering</strong>. To misalign with the calendar was to invite disorder. To sacrifice at the wrong time was to sever the link between earth and star.</p>
