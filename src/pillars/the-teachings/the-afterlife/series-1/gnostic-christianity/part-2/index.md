  <p>He said, &ldquo;You are children of the living Father.&rdquo; The bishops said, &ldquo;You are fallen and unworthy.&rdquo;</p>

  <p>He revealed a God beyond jealousy, violence, and judgement&mdash;they rebranded the Demiurge and called him holy.</p>

  <p>And so the war began&mdash;not between good and evil, but between <strong>gnosis and control</strong>.</p>

  <p>The Christ they preached? Dead. Obedient. Chained to a cross.</p>
